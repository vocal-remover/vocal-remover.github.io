What is Vocal Remover?

Vocal Remover is a focused AI vocal remover that splits any song into vocals and instrumental. It's built for real-world use: karaoke, remixes, practice, teaching and content creation.

You can:

  • Remove vocals from a song to get a clean instrumental version.
  • Isolate voice to study phrasing, harmonies and layers.
  • Prepare karaoke tracks, backing tracks and teaching material.
  • Clean up podcasts and videos by removing background music.

Vocal Remover comes in two versions: Vocal Remover Online (browser extension, for websites) and Vocal Remover Offline (Mac app, for files).
